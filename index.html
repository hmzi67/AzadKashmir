About Muzaffarabad
capital of Azad Kashmir

Muzaffarabad is the capital and largest city of Azad Kashmir, and the 60th largest in Pakistan. The city is located in Muzaffarabad District, near the confluence of the Jhelum and Neelum rivers.

About district BAGH

Bagh, Azad Kashmir

Bagh is located 80 kilometres from Muzaffarabad. Topographically, the district is quite mountainous with lush green pine forests on the mountain slopes leading into the valleys.

Facts About "Bagh"

Bagh City (باغ) is the chief town and district headquarters of Bagh District, in Azad Kashmir, Pakistan. The town is situated on the confluence of two nullahs (streams), Malwani and Mahl (ماہلوانی اور ماہل) at the distance of 93 km from Muzaffarabad.

Bagh City

Bagh has developed into a good city and trade center after creation of district headquarters in 1987. Dhirkot, Kahuta, Yadgar Huda Bhari, Fort Bagh, Sudhan Gali, Neela Butt and Darra Haji-Pir are very important places of district Bagh
